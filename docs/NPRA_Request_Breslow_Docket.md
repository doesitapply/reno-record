## Nevada Public Records Act Request

Pursuant to the **Nevada Public Records Act, NRS Chapter 239**, I hereby request access to and copies of the following public records maintained by the Second Judicial District Court, Washoe County, Nevada.

---

### Records Requested

**1. Case Docket Data — Judge Barry Breslow**

All electronic docket entries, minute orders, and case disposition records for cases assigned to **Judge Barry Breslow** (Department 6, Second Judicial District Court) for the period **January 1, 2020 through the date of this request**, including but not limited to:

- Case number
- Case filing date
- Case type (criminal, civil, family, etc.)
- Defendant/respondent representation status (pro se or represented by counsel)
- Date of each minute order or ruling
- Full text of each minute order or ruling
- Case disposition date
- Disposition type (dismissed, convicted, acquitted, settled, etc.)
- Any notation of Faretta hearings or self-representation waivers

**2. Time-to-Ruling Records**

For each case in the above date range, the timestamp (date and time, if available) of:

- When a motion was filed
- When the corresponding ruling or minute order was issued

**3. Pro Se Case Outcomes**

A summary or raw data of case outcomes (conviction rate, dismissal rate, continuance rate) broken down by whether the defendant was represented by counsel or appeared pro se, for all criminal cases in Department 6 during the above period.

**4. Appellate Reversal Records**

Any records maintained by the court reflecting cases from Department 6 that were reversed, remanded, or vacated on appeal by the Nevada Supreme Court or Nevada Court of Appeals during the period January 1, 2020 through the date of this request.

I request that records be produced in **electronic format** where available, specifically:

- Docket data in CSV, JSON, or Excel format
- Minute orders and rulings as PDF files
- Any database exports in their native structured format

If electronic production is not available for any category of records, I request paper copies.

### Legal Basis

This request is made pursuant to:

- **NRS 239.010** — All public books and records of a governmental entity, the contents of which are not otherwise declared by law to be confidential, must be open at all times during office hours to inspection by any person.
- **NRS 239.052** — Procedures for requesting inspection or copies of public records.
- **NRS 239.0107** — Governmental entities must respond within **5 business days** of receiving a written request.

Court docket entries, minute orders, and case disposition records are public records under Nevada law. *See Donrey of Nevada, Inc. v. Bradshaw*, 106 Nev. 630 (1990); *DR Partners v. Board of County Commissioners*, 116 Nev. 616 (2000).
